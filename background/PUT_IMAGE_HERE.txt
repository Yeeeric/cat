DROP YOUR BACKGROUND IMAGE IN HERE
====================================

Place any image file in this folder with one of these names:

  background.jpg
  background.jpeg
  background.png
  background.webp
  background.gif

The app will automatically use it as the room background.
If no image is found, a built-in cozy CSS room scene is shown instead.

Recommended: a cozy interior scene (living room, bedroom, cafe, etc.)
Any resolution works — the image will be scaled to fill the window.
